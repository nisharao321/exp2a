Relative humidity of whirling hygrometer

Objective

To determine the relative humidity of a location using whirling hygrometer and compare the result using psychrometric chart.


Apparatus used:

Whirling Hygrometer, Psychrometric Chart, Distilled Water, Stopwatch etc.

Description Whirling Hygrometers: have a pair of thermometers, one of them having its bulb covered with wet muslin cloth. Here dry and wet bulb thermometer are mounted side by side on bass frame fitted with a handle for whirling purpose.
